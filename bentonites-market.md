<p><p>Bentonite is a versatile clay primarily categorized into Sodium Bentonite and Calcium Bentonite based on their dominant exchangeable ions. Sodium Bentonite, known for its high swelling capacity and water absorption, is widely used in drilling fluids, foundry sands, and cat litter. Calcium Bentonite, with lower swelling properties, is commonly used in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and as a binder in iron ore pelletizing. Both types cater to diverse industries, with demand driven by their unique physical and chemical properties.</p></p>
