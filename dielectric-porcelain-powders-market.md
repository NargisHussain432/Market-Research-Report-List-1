COG has minimal capacitance drift, ideal for precise electronic components. Y5V exhibits high capacitance change with temperature fluctuations, often used in cost-sensitive applications. Other types encompass various formulations tailored for specific electrical properties and performance needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
