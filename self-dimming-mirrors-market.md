<p><strong>The Self-dimming Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Self-dimming Mirrors</li><li>Outer Self-dimming Mirrors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-dimming mirrors market is segmented into two key types: inside and outside self-dimming mirrors. Inside self-dimming mirrors are typically installed in vehicles’ interiors, automatically adjusting their tint to reduce glare from headlights behind them, enhancing driver comfort and safety. Outside self-dimming mirrors are mounted on vehicle exteriors, performing a similar function by dimming in response to incoming light, improving visibility and reducing distractions. Both types contribute to safer driving experiences by optimizing reflections in varying lighting conditions.</p></p>
