<p><p>The Dairy Products Packaging Coding System market encompasses various technologies used to print essential information on product packaging. Inkjet coding involves printing variable data, such as expiration dates and batch numbers, using ink droplets, ensuring flexibility and speed. Laser coding, on the other hand, utilizes focused laser beams to etch codes onto packaging materials, offering high durability and precision without ink. Other techniques may include thermal transfer and dot peen coding, catering to specific dairy product requirements and enhancing traceability and compliance.</p></p>
