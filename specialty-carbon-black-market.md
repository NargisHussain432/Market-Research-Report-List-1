<p><ul><li>Lamp Black</li><li>Acetylene Black</li><li>Gas Black</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty carbon black market encompasses various types, including Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, Gas Black, and others. Lamp Black is produced by incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, offering excellent black pigmentation and is commonly used in inks and coatings. Acetylene Black is derived from the thermal decomposition of acetylene gas, known for its high purity and high electrical conductivity, making it ideal for batteries and conductive materials. Gas Black is produced from gas-phase processes, providing unique properties for specific applications. Other types include various specialty grades catering to niche markets.</p></p>
