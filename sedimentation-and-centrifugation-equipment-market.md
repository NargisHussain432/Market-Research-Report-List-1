<p><p>The sedimentation and centrifugation equipment market includes various types designed for separating solids from liquids. Clarifiers utilize gravity to settle solids, while disk centrifuges employ high-speed rotation to enhance separation efficiency. Decanters continuously separate solids from liquids using centrifugal force. Dissolved air flotation systems introduce air to promote solid separation through buoyancy. Hydrocyclones use centrifugal force to separate particles based on size and density. Other market variants may include filters and skimmers, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
