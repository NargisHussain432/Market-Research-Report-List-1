<p><p>Auto-transformers are electrical devices used to step up or step down voltage levels in power systems. They consist of a single winding that serves as both the primary and secondary winding, resulting in a more compact and efficient design compared to traditional transformers. This unique configuration allows for significant reduction in material costs and weight, making them popular in various applications, including power generation, distribution, and electrical railways.</p><p>The Auto-transformers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period. <p><p>The auto-transformer market is categorized into two main types: single-phase and three-phase auto-transformers.
