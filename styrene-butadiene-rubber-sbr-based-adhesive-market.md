<p><p>The Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (SBR) based adhesive market comprises two primary types: solution types and emulsion types. Solution-based adhesives utilize SBR dissolved in organic solvents, offering strong bonding properties suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, emulsion types consist of SBR dispersed in water, providing an easier application process and lower environmental impact. Both types are widely used in various sectors, including automotive, construction, and textiles, due to their excellent adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to heat and chemicals.</p></p>
