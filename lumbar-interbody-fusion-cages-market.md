<p><ul><li>Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion Cage</li><li>Terior Lumbar Interbody Fusion Cage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lumbar Interbody Fusion Cages market is primarily divided into two main types: Posterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (PLIF) cages and Anterior Lumbar Interbody Fusion (ALIF) cages. PLIF cages are inserted through the back, providing stabilizing support to the spine and allowing for access to the intervertebral disc. In contrast, ALIF cages are placed from the front, offering a larger area for fusion and minimal disruption to the back muscles. Both types enhance spinal stability and promote healing in lumbar fusion procedures.</p></p>
