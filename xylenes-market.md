<p><p>The xylene market comprises four main types: ortho xylene, meta xylene, para xylene, and mixed xylene. Ortho xylene is primarily used in the production of phthalate plasticizers and solvents. Meta xylene finds applications in producing isophthalic acid, while para xylene is crucial for manufacturing terephthalic acid, a key component in polyester production. Mixed xylene, a blend of the three isomers, serves as a solvent and chemical feedstock, making it essential across various industrial applications, including coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
