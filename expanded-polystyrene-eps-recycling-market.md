<p><p>The Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) recycling market is divided into three primary types: material recycling, chemical recycling, and thermal recycling. Material recycling involves mechanically processing EPS to create new products, reducing waste. Chemical recycling breaks down EPS into its original monomers, allowing for the creation of virgin-like materials. Thermal recycling involves incinerating EPS to recover energy, though it can generate emissions. Each method aims to promote sustainability by repurposing EPS waste, minimizing environmental impact, and conserving resources.</p></p>
