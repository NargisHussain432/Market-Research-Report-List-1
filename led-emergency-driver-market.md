<p><p>The LED Emergency Driver market is categorized into two primary types: external DC fuse and no-external DC fuse. </p><p>External DC fuse drivers incorporate an additional protection mechanism against overcurrent, enhancing safety during emergency situations. This type is commonly used in applications where reliability is a priority. </p><p>Conversely, no-external DC fuse drivers lack this extra protection, streamlining installation and reducing complexity. They are often favored for cost-sensitive projects or in environments where space is limited. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring reliable LED performance during emergencies.</p></p>
