<p><p>The retail casters market primarily includes swivel and rigid casters, each serving distinct functions. Swivel casters feature a wheel that can rotate 360 degrees, allowing for enhanced maneuverability and ease of movement in tight spaces, making them ideal for shopping carts and display stands. Rigid casters, on the other hand, allow movement in a straight line and provide stability, often used for heavier items like shelving units and workstations. Both types are essential for optimizing logistics and functionality in retail environments.</p></p>
