<p><p>Ultrasonic NDT testing equipment encompasses various market types, including flaw detectors and thickness gauges. Thickness gauges, on the other hand, measure the thickness of materials, crucial for assessing wear or corrosion levels. Additionally, other ultrasonic equipment may include surface wave testers or transit time instruments, which serve niche applications in material evaluation and inspection, providing versatile solutions across industries.</p></p>
