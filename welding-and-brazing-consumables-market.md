<p><p>The Welding and Brazing Consumables market encompasses two primary types: welding consumables and brazing consumables. Welding consumables include materials like electrodes and filler metals used in various welding processes to join metals through fusion. In contrast, brazing consumables involve filler materials that melt at higher temperatures but below the melting point of the base metals, enabling the creation of strong joints without melting the base materials. Both sectors are essential for ensuring quality and efficiency in manufacturing and construction applications.</p></p>
