<p><p>The bunker fuel market primarily comprises three types: Distillate Fuel Oil (DFO), Residual Fuel Oil (RFO), and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG). DFO is a cleaner, lighter fuel used in smaller vessels and to comply with stricter emissions regulations. RFO, a heavier and more viscous fuel, is often used in larger ships but contains higher emissions. LNG is gaining popularity due to its lower environmental impact, providing an alternative fuel source that significantly reduces sulfur oxides and particulate emissions.</p></p>
