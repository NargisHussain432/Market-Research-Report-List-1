<p><p>Contact IC cards are divided into two main types: memory cards and microprocessor cards. Memory cards primarily store data and have limited processing capabilities, making them suitable for simple applications like access control or prepaid services. In contrast, microprocessor cards feature a built-in processor, enabling them to perform more complex tasks, including secure transactions and enhanced data management. These cards typically support advanced encryption and authentication mechanisms, making them ideal for applications like banking, healthcare, and identification systems.</p></p>
