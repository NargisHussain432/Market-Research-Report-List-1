<p><p>LED Through Beam Photoelectric Sensors are categorized into two main market types based on the signal output: Digital Signal and Analog Signal. Digital Signal sensors provide binary outputs, indicating either a presence or absence of an object, making them ideal for precise detection applications. In contrast, Analog Signal sensors offer continuous output proportional to the distance or intensity of the detected object, allowing for more nuanced measurements and control in applications requiring detailed feedback. Both types enhance automation efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
