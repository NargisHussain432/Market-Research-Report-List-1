<p><ul><li>Whole-Home</li><li>Split Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water softener installation market can be divided into two main types: whole-home systems and split systems. Whole-home water softeners treat all water entering a residence, providing comprehensive softening for every faucet and appliance. This system is ideal for households with high water usage. In contrast, split systems consist of point-of-use softeners that target specific fixtures, such as showers or sinks, allowing for more tailored solutions while often being more cost-effective for smaller households or selective applications.</p></p>
