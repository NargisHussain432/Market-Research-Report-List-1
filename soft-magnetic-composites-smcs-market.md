<p><p>Soft Magnetic Composites (SMCs) are advanced materials designed to improve the efficiency of magnetic applications. These composites, made by combining soft magnetic powders with an insulating binder, offer significant advantages over traditional laminated magnetic materials, including enhanced magnetic properties, reduced eddy current losses, and greater design flexibility. SMCs are widely utilized in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics, particularly in electric motors, transformers, and inductors.</p><p>The market for Soft Magnetic Composites is experiencing robust growth, fueled by the increasing demand for energy-efficient electromagnetic devices and the rise of electric vehicles.
