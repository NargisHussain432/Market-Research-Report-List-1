<p><p>Aspirator ion pumps are crucial in creating high-vacuum environments in various applications. The market for these pumps mainly includes two types: evaporator ion pumps and sputtering ion pumps. Evaporator ion pumps utilize the evaporation of materials to create ions that are then trapped to maintain vacuum, while sputtering ion pumps use a process where energetic particles collide with a target material, ejecting ions to achieve a similar effect. Both types are essential for industries like semiconductor manufacturing and scientific research.</p></p>
