<p><ul><li>Twin-tube Type</li><li>Mono-tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle original equipment (OE) shock absorbers market primarily consists of two types: twin-tube and mono-tube. Twin-tube shock absorbers feature an inner and outer cylinder, providing effective damping with good ride comfort, often utilized in budget and standard vehicles. Conversely, mono-tube shock absorbers have a single cylinder design, offering superior heat dissipation and performance, making them popular in higher-end vehicles and those requiring enhanced handling. Both types cater to varying consumer needs and preferences in suspension performance.</p></p>
