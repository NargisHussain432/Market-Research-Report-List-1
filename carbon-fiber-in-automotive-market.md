<p><p>Carbon fiber in the automotive market includes various materials and manufacturing types. Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) combines resin and carbon fibers for strong, lightweight parts. Short Fiber Thermoplastic (SFT) utilizes short carbon fibers in thermoplastics for improved processing and cost-effectiveness. Long Fiber Thermoplastic (LFT) offers enhanced mechanical properties due to longer fibers. Prepreg involves carbon fibers pre-impregnated with resin for high-performance applications. Other market types may include hybrid composites or specific formulations tailored for unique automotive needs, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
