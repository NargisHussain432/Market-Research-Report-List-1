<p><p>An Optical Parametric Amplifier (OPA) is a device that enhances optical signals using nonlinear optical processes. The OPA market can be divided into two main types: Tunable Wavelength Amplifiers and Fixed Wavelength Amplifiers. Tunable Wavelength Amplifiers can adjust their output wavelengths, making them versatile for various applications across telecommunications and spectroscopy. Fixed Wavelength Amplifiers, on the other hand, operate at a specific wavelength, providing stable amplification for applications requiring consistency, such as in sensing technologies and fiber optic communications.</p></p>
