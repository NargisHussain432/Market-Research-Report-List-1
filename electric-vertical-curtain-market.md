<p><strong>The Electric Vertical Curtain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Remote Control</li><li>Radio Remote Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vertical Curtain market features two primary control types: Infrared Remote Control and Radio Remote Control. Infrared Remote Control operates through direct line-of-sight communication, requiring the user to point the remote at the curtain mechanism. This method is simple but limited in range and mobility. In contrast, Radio Remote Control utilizes radio waves, allowing control from greater distances and through obstacles. This versatility makes it increasingly popular, as users can operate their curtains from anywhere within the range, enhancing convenience and functionality.</p></p>
