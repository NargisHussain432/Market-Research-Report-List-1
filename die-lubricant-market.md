<p><strong>The Die Lubricant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lubricating Wax</li><li>Lubricating Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die lubricant market comprises two primary types: lubricating wax and lubricating oil. Lubricating wax is typically used in processes requiring a solid lubricant, providing excellent thermal stability and reducing friction in high-temperature applications. Lubricating oil, on the other hand, is a liquid lubricant that offers superior flow characteristics, allowing for better coverage and cooling during die operations. Both types enhance the longevity of dies and improve production efficiency by minimizing wear and tear on equipment.</p></p>
