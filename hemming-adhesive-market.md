<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hemming Adhesive market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and are ready for use without any additional preparation, providing convenience and efficiency. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and a hardener before application, allowing for stronger bonds and greater versatility in various conditions. Both types are widely used in automotive manufacturing for assembling parts, offering solutions tailored to specific bonding needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
