<p><p>The Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) microsphere market is primarily categorized into hollow and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are lightweight and primarily used for applications requiring low density and specific buoyancy, such as in the aerospace and automotive industries. Both types capitalize on PTFE's unique chemical resistance and thermal stability, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
