<p><p>The automotive torque converter market comprises two primary types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters feature a simple design with one set of turbine and impeller, providing smooth power transfer and efficiency for standard vehicles. In contrast, multistage torque converters incorporate multiple sets of turbine and impeller stages, allowing for better performance and efficiency under varying loads, making them suitable for high-performance and heavy-duty applications. Both types play critical roles in enhancing vehicle drivability and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
