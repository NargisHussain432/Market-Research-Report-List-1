<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart thermal cameras market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave cameras. Short-wave length cameras, operating in the 0.9 to 3 micrometers range, capture heat emissions from various surfaces, ideal for industrial applications. Mid-wave length cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are suited for detecting temperature variations in complex environments, commonly used in surveillance. Long-wave length cameras, operating from 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in low-light conditions, often used for building inspections and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
