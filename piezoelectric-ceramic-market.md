<p><p>The piezoelectric ceramic market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT) are widely used due to their high electromechanical coupling and versatility in sensors and actuators. Lead Titanate (PT) offers excellent piezoelectric properties, making it suitable for applications in transducers and motors. Lead Magnesium Niobate (PMN) is prized for its high sensitivity and is often used in advanced medical imaging. Other materials may include polymer-based or composite piezoelectric ceramics, catering to specialized needs across various industries.</p></p>
