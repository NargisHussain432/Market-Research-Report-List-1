<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Turbine Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jockey pumps market comprises two primary types: centrifugal pumps and turbine pumps. Centrifugal pumps operate by converting rotational kinetic energy into hydraulic energy, making them suitable for maintaining pressure in fire protection systems. They are efficient and widely used. Turbine pumps, on the other hand, utilize a series of impellers to move water vertically, making them ideal for applications requiring high pressure and flow rates. Both types play critical roles in ensuring consistent water supply and pressure in various systems.</p></p>
