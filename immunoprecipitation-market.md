<p><p>The immunoprecipitation market includes several key types: Individual IP, Coimmunoprecipitation, and ChIP (chromatin immunoprecipitation). Individual IP focuses on isolating specific proteins from complex mixtures using antibodies. Coimmunoprecipitation examines protein-protein interactions by capturing complexes of proteins that bind together. ChIP specifically investigates protein-DNA interactions, allowing researchers to study gene regulation. Each type serves distinct research needs in fields like cell biology, molecular biology, and genetics, driving advancements in scientific understanding and therapeutic development.</p></p>
