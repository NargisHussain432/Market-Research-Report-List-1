<p><p>The dental glue market encompasses two main types: temporary and permanent dental glue. Temporary dental glue is designed for short-term use, often for holding dental appliances or crowns until a permanent solution is applied, providing flexibility and ease of removal. In contrast, permanent dental glue is formulated for long-lasting adhesion, ensuring durability for restorations and prosthetics. Both types cater to different dental needs, with temporary options focusing on convenience and permanence prioritizing lasting results in dental procedures.</p></p>
