<p><ul><li>Fermented</li><li>Unfermented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen bread dough market is divided into two main types: fermented and unfermented. Fermented frozen dough undergoes a proofing process before freezing, allowing for enhanced flavor and texture after baking. It is often preferred by bakers seeking quality and convenience. Unfermented frozen dough, on the other hand, is simply mixed and frozen without fermentation, offering a quicker option for those needing immediate baking solutions. Both types cater to varying customer preferences in the commercial and retail sectors.</p></p>
