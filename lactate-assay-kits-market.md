<p><p>Lactate assay kits are essential tools for measuring lactate levels in various samples, with two primary market types: colorimetric and fluorometric. Colorimetric assays utilize color changes to quantify lactate concentrations, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, fluorometric assays employ fluorescence detection, providing higher sensitivity and specificity, ideal for low-lactate samples. Both types cater to diverse research and clinical applications, enabling accurate lactate monitoring in fields such as sports medicine, diagnostics, and metabolic research.</p></p>
