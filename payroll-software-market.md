<p><p>Payroll software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based payroll software operates online, allowing businesses to access their payroll data from anywhere with internet connectivity. It typically offers subscription-based pricing and automatic updates, enhancing flexibility and scalability. On-premises payroll software, on the other hand, is installed locally on company servers. This type provides full control over data and security but requires significant upfront investment and maintenance efforts. Both types cater to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
