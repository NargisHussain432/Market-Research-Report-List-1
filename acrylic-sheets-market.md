<p><strong>The Acrylic Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic sheets market primarily comprises two types: extruded and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced by forcing acrylic resin through a mold, resulting in uniform thickness and a cost-effective option ideal for sheet applications and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are made by pouring liquid acrylic into a mold, yielding superior clarity, thickness options, and greater resistance to UV light and weathering. These two types cater to diverse applications in signage, lighting, and construction industries.</p></p>
