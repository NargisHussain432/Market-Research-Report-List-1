<p><p>The automotive imaging radar sensors market comprises three primary types: Short-Range Radar (SSR), Mid-Range Radar (MRR), and Long-Range Radar (LRR). SSR typically operates within a range of up to 50 meters, aiding in close-proximity applications like parking assistance. MRR operates between 30 to 150 meters, useful for adaptive cruise control and lane change applications. LRR, with a range exceeding 150 meters, supports advanced features such as collision avoidance and autonomous driving functions, ensuring comprehensive vehicle awareness and safety.</p></p>
