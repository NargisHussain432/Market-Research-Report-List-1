<p><p>The linear array image sensor market consists primarily of three types: CMOS, CCD, and others. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors are known for their low power consumption and high integration capabilities, making them popular in portable devices. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors deliver superior image quality and sensitivity, often used in professional applications. Other types encompass various specialized sensors designed for niche applications, including infrared and single-photon sensors, catering to diverse industrial, medical, and scientific imaging needs.</p></p>
