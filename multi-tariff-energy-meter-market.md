<p><p>The multi-tariff energy meter market is segmented into single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase meters are typically used in residential applications, measuring energy consumption for lower power needs, generally up to 230V. In contrast, three-phase meters cater to commercial and industrial users, handling higher loads and providing greater efficiency for operations requiring more power, typically exceeding 400V. Both types enable users to benefit from various tariff rates based on peak and off-peak consumption, promoting energy conservation and cost savings.</p></p>
