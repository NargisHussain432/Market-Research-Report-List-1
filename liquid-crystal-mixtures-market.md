<p><p>The liquid crystal mixtures market comprises several types, each catering to specific display technologies. TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) liquid crystal mixtures are used in high-resolution displays, offering faster response times and better color reproduction. TN (Twisted Nematic) liquid crystal mixtures are known for their cost-effectiveness and are commonly found in budget displays, though they have limited viewing angles. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) liquid crystal mixtures provide improved contrast and are often used in monochrome displays. Other types include specific blends for niche applications.</p></p>
