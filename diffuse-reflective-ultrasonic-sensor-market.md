<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diffuse reflective ultrasonic sensors are classified into two main types based on their output: analog and digital. Analog output sensors provide continuous signals that represent the distance to an object, allowing for smooth variations in readings. In contrast, digital output sensors emit discrete signals, indicating specific thresholds or conditions, such as object presence or absence. Both types are crucial in applications like automation, robotics, and distance measurement, offering versatility in how data is processed and utilized in various systems.</p></p>
