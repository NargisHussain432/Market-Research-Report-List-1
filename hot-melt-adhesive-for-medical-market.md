<p><p>Hot melt adhesives (HMAs) are thermoplastic materials used in medical applications for their strong bonding properties and versatility. SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) hot melt adhesives offer excellent elasticity and resistance, making them suitable for flexible medical devices and wound dressings. SIS (Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene) hot melt adhesives provide enhanced softness and good adhesion to various substrates, ideal for sensitive applications like skin contact products. Both types are essential in the medical market for manufacturing durable, skin-friendly, and effective medical solutions.</p></p>
