<p><p>The dental implant wrenches market includes two primary types: ratchet dental implant wrenches and torque dental implant wrenches. Ratchet wrenches are designed for quick, repetitive actions, allowing dental professionals to place implants efficiently with minimal hand fatigue. Torque wrenches, on the other hand, provide precise control over the amount of force applied during implant placement, ensuring optimal stability and preventing damage to the implant or surrounding structures. Both types enhance the accuracy and effectiveness of dental implant procedures.</p></p>
