<p><p>The engineering adhesives and sealant products market comprises several types based on their composition and application. Water-soluble adhesives are easily cleanable and environmentally friendly, making them ideal for temporary bonds. Water emulsion adhesives combine water and polymer, offering versatility and low toxicity. Solvent-based adhesives provide strong bonds and durability but may have environmental concerns due to solvents. Solid-state adhesives, often in a thermoplastic or thermosetting form, provide robust performance across various industrial applications, including construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
