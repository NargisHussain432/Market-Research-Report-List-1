<p><p>Automotive Dealer Management Systems (DMS) are integrated software solutions that streamline dealership operations, including sales, inventory management, and customer relationship management. The market comprises two main types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based DMS are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs, making them ideal for dealerships seeking lower initial investments. In contrast, on-premise DMS are installed locally, providing more control over data security and customization but often requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
