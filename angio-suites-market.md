<p><p>Angio suites are specialized facilities designed for angiographic procedures, with two primary types: biplane and single plane angio suites. Biplane angio suites feature dual imaging systems, allowing simultaneous capture of images from two angles, enhancing precision for complex procedures such as interventional cardiology. Single plane angio suites utilize a single imaging system, which is generally sufficient for less intricate procedures. Both types cater to specific clinical needs, impacting their design, equipment requirements, and overall market demand in healthcare settings.</p></p>
