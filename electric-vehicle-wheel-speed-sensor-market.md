<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises mainly two types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type sensors. Hall Type sensors utilize the Hall effect to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed measurements. They are known for their reliability and durability in varying conditions. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on magnetic fields generated by rotating components to determine speed. This type is cost-effective and simpler in design, making it suitable for various electric vehicle applications, ensuring optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
