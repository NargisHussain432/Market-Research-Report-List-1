<p><strong>The Gas Wall-hung Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instant Type</li><li>Volumetric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas wall-hung boiler market can be categorized into two main types: instant type and volumetric type. Instant type boilers provide hot water on demand, rapidly heating water as it flows through the unit, making them ideal for smaller spaces or when immediate hot water is required. In contrast, volumetric type boilers store a specific volume of hot water, allowing for a consistent supply but requiring more space. Each type caters to different consumer needs based on efficiency, space, and hot water demand.</p></p>
