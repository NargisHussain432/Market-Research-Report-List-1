<p><p>Float level switches are essential for detecting liquid levels in various applications. The market is categorized into three types: top-mounted, side-mounted, and bottom-mounted. Top-mounted type switches are installed on the tank's top, providing easy access for maintenance. Side-mounted types are attached to the side of tanks, suitable for smaller spaces and offering a reliable operation. Bottom-mounted types are positioned at the tank's base, ideal for deeper tanks, ensuring accurate measurements for low-level detection. Each type serves unique installation and application needs.</p></p>
