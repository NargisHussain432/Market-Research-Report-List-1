<p><p>Foamed polypropylene films are categorized based on thickness: up to 80 microns, 81-120 microns, and above 120 microns. Films up to 80 microns are typically lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for packaging applications. The 81-120 microns range offers enhanced durability and barrier properties, suitable for protective packaging and applications requiring moderate strength. Films above 120 microns are thicker and more robust, often used in demanding environments, including construction and automotive industries, where superior impact resistance is essential.</p></p>
