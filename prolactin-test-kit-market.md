<p><strong>The Prolactin Test Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time-resolved Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Prolactin Test Kit market includes two main types: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes fluorescent markers to detect prolactin levels with high sensitivity and specificity, offering quick results. In contrast, CLIA employs chemical luminescence to measure hormone concentrations, known for its accuracy and robustness in clinical settings. Both methods are essential for diagnosing conditions related to prolactin imbalances, catering to healthcare providers by enhancing patient care through efficient testing.</p></p>
