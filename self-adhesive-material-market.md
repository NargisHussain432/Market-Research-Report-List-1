<p><p>The self-adhesive material market primarily consists of two types: rubber-based and acrylic-based adhesives. Rubber-type adhesives are known for their strong initial tack and flexibility, making them ideal for applications requiring high adhesion and resistance to aging. In contrast, acrylic-type adhesives offer superior UV stability, making them suitable for outdoor use and longer-lasting applications. Both types are widely used in labels, tapes, and various industries, catering to specific performance requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
