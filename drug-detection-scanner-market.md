<p><p>The drug detection scanner market comprises various technologies tailored for identifying illicit substances. Gamma-ray radiography technology uses high-energy beams to penetrate materials, detecting hidden drugs through varying absorption rates. X-ray radiography technology employs lower energy radiation, providing detailed images of objects for drug identification. Muon tomography technology leverages cosmic muons to analyze density variations within objects, effectively revealing concealed substances. Together, these methods enhance security and efficiency in drug detection across various applications such as border control and law enforcement.</p></p>
