<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wave Energy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oscillating Water Column</li><li>Oscillating Body Converters</li><li>Overtopping Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wave energy market features several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Oscillating Water Column (OWC)** uses the rise and fall of waves to compress air, generating electricity via turbines.</p><p>2. **Oscillating Body Converters** employ floating structures that move with wave motion, directly converting mechanical energy into electrical power.</p><p>3. **Overtopping Converters** capture water from waves that overtop a structure, storing it in a reservoir and using hydro turbines to produce energy. Each type offers unique advantages and efficiencies in harnessing marine energy.</p></p>
