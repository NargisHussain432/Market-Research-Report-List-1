<p><ul><li>Electric Powered</li><li>Air Powered</li><li>Ultrasonic Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary dental handpieces market is primarily categorized into three types: electric powered, air powered, and ultrasonic powered handpieces. Electric powered handpieces offer consistent torque and speed, allowing for efficient dental procedures. Air powered handpieces rely on compressed air, providing high speed for various tasks but may lack the torque of electric models. Ultrasonic powered handpieces utilize high-frequency vibrations to aid in scaling and cleaning, making them ideal for precise dental work. Each type serves specific needs in veterinary dentistry.</p></p>
