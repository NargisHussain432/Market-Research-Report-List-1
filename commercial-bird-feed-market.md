<p><p>The commercial bird feed market is generally divided into two primary categories: staple and supplement feeds. Staple feeds serve as the primary diet for birds, providing essential nutrients, calories, and energy needed for maintenance and growth. These typically include formulations like pellets or seeds designed for specific bird species. Supplement feeds, on the other hand, enhance the nutritional value of the staple diet, offering specialized vitamins, minerals, or proteins to support health, breeding, and overall well-being. Together, they ensure a balanced diet for birds.</p></p>
