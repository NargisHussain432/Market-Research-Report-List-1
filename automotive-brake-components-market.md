<p><p>The automotive brake components market can be categorized into three main types: drum brakes, disc brakes, and other brake systems. Drum brakes consist of a hollow drum that rotates with the wheel, using friction to slow down the vehicle. Disc brakes feature a rotating disc and calipers that grip it for efficient stopping power and heat dissipation. Other brake systems may include advanced technologies like regenerative braking or air brakes used in specific vehicle applications, enhancing overall braking performance and safety.</p></p>
