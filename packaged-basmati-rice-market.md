<p><p>The packaged basmati rice market comprises two primary types: dry basmati rice and parboiled basmati rice. Dry basmati rice refers to the unprocessed grains that require cooking, retaining their aromatic flavor and long grains. In contrast, parboiled basmati rice undergoes a steaming process before milling, enhancing its nutritional profile and reducing cooking time. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with dry rice appealing to traditional cooks and parboiled rice attracting those seeking convenience without sacrificing quality.</p></p>
