<p><p>The glass lenses market encompasses various types tailored to different vision needs. Progressive lenses provide a seamless transition between multiple vision zones, catering to those needing multiple prescriptions. Single vision lenses are designed for one prescription, primarily for distance or near vision, making them ideal for straightforward eyesight correction. Bifocal lenses feature two distinct optical regions for distant and near vision, offering a traditional solution for presbyopia. Together, these lens types address diverse visual requirements in the eyewear industry.</p></p>
