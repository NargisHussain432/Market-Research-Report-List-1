<p><ul><li>Point Sample Test Based Glucose Monitors</li><li>CGM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetes monitors market comprises two primary types: point sample test-based glucose monitors and continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems. Point sample test monitors provide single glucose readings using fingerstick blood samples, offering patients immediate results. In contrast, CGM systems continuously track glucose levels throughout the day and night, providing real-time data and alerts for fluctuations. Both types play a vital role in diabetes management, catering to different patient needs and preferences by enabling better control of blood sugar levels.</p></p>
