<p><strong>The Animal Pregnancy Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Wave</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Animal pregnancy detectors primarily fall into two categories: ultrasonic wave detectors and other types. Ultrasonic wave detectors utilize sound waves to examine the fetus's presence and development within the animal's reproductive system, offering a non-invasive and accurate method for confirming pregnancy in various livestock. Other market types may include hormonal assays or visual inspection methods, which rely on observable physiological changes or chemical analysis to determine pregnancy status. Each method varies in precision, cost, and applicability depending on the species.</p></p>
