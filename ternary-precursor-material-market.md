<p><p>The ternary precursor material market is primarily categorized into two types: NCM (Nickel Cobalt Manganese) and NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum). NCM precursors are used in lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles, offering a balanced performance with improved thermal stability and energy density. Conversely, NCA precursors provide higher energy density and better capacity retention but can experience thermal instability. Both types are essential for enhancing battery efficiency, with applications in electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
