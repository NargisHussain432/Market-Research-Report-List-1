<p><ul><li>Powder Bed</li><li>Blown Powder</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal powders for additive manufacturing are categorized into several types based on the printing technique employed. Powder bed fusion involves layers of powder being selectively melted by a laser or electron beam, ideal for complex geometries. Blown powder techniques, like directed energy deposition, use a focused energy source to fuse metal powders as they are blown onto a substrate, offering flexibility in part repair and high deposition rates. Other methods include binder jetting and sheet lamination, expanding the scope of applications and materials used in manufacturing.</p></p>
