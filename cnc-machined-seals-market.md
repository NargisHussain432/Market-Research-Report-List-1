<p><p>CNC machined seals encompass various types designed for specific applications. Piston seals ensure a tight fit within cylinders; rod seals prevent fluid leakage along moving rods. Rotary seals are used in rotating machinery to maintain pressure and exclude contaminants. Guide rings support dynamic seals and reduce friction, while backup rings provide extra reinforcement to prevent extrusion. Gaskets effectively seal surfaces and prevent leaks. Other market types include specialized seals tailored for unique requirements across different industries, enhancing functionality and reliability.</p></p>
