<p><strong>The Scuba Diving Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Respiratory Regulators</li><li>Closed Respiratory Regulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scuba diving regulators market is primarily divided into two types: open respiratory regulators and closed respiratory regulators. Open respiratory regulators allow exhaled air to escape into the surrounding water, promoting simplicity and ease of use, making them popular among recreational divers. In contrast, closed respiratory regulators recycle exhaled air, providing enhanced gas conservation and extended dive times, making them ideal for technical and deep-sea divers. Each type caters to specific diving needs and preferences, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
