<p><strong>The Infusion Bottle Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP-H</li><li>PP-R</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infusion Bottle Polypropylene market is primarily categorized into two types: PP-H (homopolymer) and PP-R (random copolymer). PP-H is known for its rigidity and excellent clarity, making it suitable for transparent infusion bottles. On the other hand, PP-R offers enhanced impact resistance and flexibility, which can be advantageous in various medical applications. Both types of polypropylene are chemically resistant and safe for medical use, driving their demand in the healthcare sector, particularly for infusion and pharmaceutical packaging.</p></p>
