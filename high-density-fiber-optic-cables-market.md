<p><p>The High-Density Fiber Optic Cable market is primarily segmented into two types: Singlemode and Multimode cables. Singlemode cables are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to reduce signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and high-speed networks. In contrast, Multimode cables support multiple light paths and are suited for shorter distances, commonly used in data centers and local area networks. Each type addresses specific bandwidth and distance requirements, influencing their applications in modern connectivity solutions.</p></p>
