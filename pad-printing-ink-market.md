<p><ul><li>One-Component Ink</li><li>Two-Component Ink</li><li>Dry Ink</li><li>Oxidation Ink</li><li>Sublimation Ink</li><li>Special Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pad printing ink market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. One-component inks are ready-to-use without mixing, while two-component inks require a hardener for optimal performance. Dry inks are specially formulated for fast drying, enhancing production efficiency. Oxidation inks cure through oxygen exposure, offering durability. Sublimation inks are designed for transferring dye onto materials, ideal for textiles. Special inks are customized for unique needs, including glow-in-the-dark or conductive properties, catering to diverse printing requirements across industries.</p></p>
