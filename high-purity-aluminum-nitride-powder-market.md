<p><p>The high-purity aluminum nitride powder market comprises various production methods, primarily including the direct nitridation method and the carbothermal reduction and nitridation method. The direct nitridation method involves reacting aluminum with nitrogen at elevated temperatures, resulting in high-purity powder. Conversely, the carbothermal reduction method uses carbon as a reducing agent, facilitating the reaction of aluminum oxide with nitrogen. Both methods cater to the demand for high-performance ceramics and semiconductors, driven by applications in electronics, aerospace, and automotive industries.</p></p>
