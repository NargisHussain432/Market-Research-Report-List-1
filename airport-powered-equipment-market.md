<p><p>The Airport Powered Equipment market encompasses various essential machines that facilitate aircraft operations. Refuelers are designed to supply fuel to aircraft efficiently. Tugs and tractors assist in moving planes on the ground. Ground Power Units provide electrical power to parked aircraft. Air Start Units (ASUs) supply compressed air to start engines. Container loaders are used for loading and unloading cargo, while transporters move baggage and cargo across the tarmac. The "Others" category includes any additional specialized equipment used at airports.</p></p>
