<p><p>The bullet-proof glass market is categorized into two main types: unidirectional and two-way. Unidirectional bullet-proof glass is designed to protect from threats coming from one side, often used in security applications like bank teller windows or armored vehicles. Conversely, two-way bullet-proof glass offers protection from both sides, making it ideal for environments requiring visibility and security from both the inside and outside, such as police stations or military installations. Both types cater to different protective needs based on their applications.</p></p>
