<p><p>The expansion joint market includes various types designed to accommodate movement in piping systems. Axial expansion joints handle linear thermal expansion, while angular expansion joints allow for angular misalignment. Lateral expansion joints manage lateral movements, providing flexibility in multiple directions. Universal expansion joints combine axial and lateral capabilities, facilitating complex movements. Each type serves crucial roles in maintaining system integrity, reducing stress on pipes, and preventing damage in various applications, including industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
