<p><strong>The Single-Use Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography</li><li>Affinity Membrane Chromatography</li><li>Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-use chromatography market is segmented into several key types: Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography, which utilizes charged membranes to separate biomolecules based on their ionic interactions; Affinity Membrane Chromatography, designed to selectively capture target molecules through specific binding interactions; and Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography, which leverages hydrophobic interactions for separating proteins and other biomolecules. These techniques enhance bioprocess efficiency, reduce contamination risks, and simplify the manufacturing process in biopharmaceutical production, making them increasingly popular in varied applications.</p></p>
