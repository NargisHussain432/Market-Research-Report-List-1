<p><p>Precision harvesting machines are classified into three main types: combines, harvesting robots, and forage harvesters. Combines are versatile machines designed for efficient grain harvesting and processing in a single operation. Harvesting robots utilize advanced automation and sensors to pick fruits and vegetables with high accuracy, minimizing waste. Forage harvesters specialize in cutting and processing fodder crops for livestock feed, ensuring optimal preservation of nutrients. Each type enhances agricultural efficiency and productivity through precision technology.</p></p>
