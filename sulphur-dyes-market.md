<p><ul><li>Powdered Sulphur Dyes</li><li>Solubilized Sulphur Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulphur dyes market comprises two primary types: powdered sulphur dyes and solubilized sulphur dyes. Powdered sulphur dyes are utilized for dyeing cotton and other cellulose-based fibers, providing vibrant colors with excellent wash and light fastness. In contrast, solubilized sulphur dyes are water-soluble and primarily used for dyeing synthetic fibers, allowing for easier application and processing. Both types cater to diverse industries, including textiles, and are favored for their cost-effectiveness and environmental compatibility, supporting sustainable practices in dyeing processes.</p></p>
