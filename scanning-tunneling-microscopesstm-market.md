<p><strong>The Scanning Tunneling Microscopes（STM） Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant-height Mode</li><li>Constant-current Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scanning Tunneling Microscopes (STM) enable imaging surfaces at the atomic level by measuring tunneling current between a conductive tip and the surface. The market bifurcates into constant-height and constant-current modes. In constant-height mode, the tip maintains a fixed distance from the surface, suitable for analyzing surface topography when variations are minimal. Conversely, constant-current mode adjusts the tip's height to maintain a steady tunneling current, making it ideal for measuring varying surfaces, ensuring precise imaging across diverse materials.</p></p>
