Metal-organic photoinitiators involve coordinated metal complexes that enhance the efficiency of polymerization reactions. Organosilane photoinitiators combine organic and silicone components, offering improved adhesion and flexibility in applications. Each type serves specific formulations, enhancing performance across industries like coatings, inks, and adhesives.</p></p>
