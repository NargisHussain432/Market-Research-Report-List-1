<p><p>Commercial fertilizer spreaders are categorized into four main types: carriable, walk-behind, ride-on, and trailer-mounted. Carriable spreaders are lightweight and portable, suitable for small areas. Walk-behind spreaders allow operators to control application while walking, ideal for medium-sized lawns. Ride-on spreaders offer comfort and efficiency for larger landscapes, enabling quick coverage. Trailer-mounted spreaders attach to vehicles for extensive agricultural use, providing high capacity and dispersal efficiency across large fields or farms, meeting diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
