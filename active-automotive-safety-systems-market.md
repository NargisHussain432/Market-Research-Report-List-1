<p><p>The Active Automotive Safety Systems market encompasses various technologies designed to enhance vehicle safety. Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) prevent wheel lock-up during hard braking, ensuring better control. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) helps maintain vehicle stability during maneuvers. Blind Spot Detection (BSD) alerts drivers of vehicles in their blind spots, improving lane-change safety. Lane Departure Warning Systems (LDWS) notify drivers when drifting out of lanes, while Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) monitor tire pressure to prevent accidents caused by under-inflated tires.</p></p>
