<p><p>Compounding Aseptic Containment Isolators (CACIs) are designed to provide a sterile environment for the preparation of hazardous medications. The market is divided into two primary types: recirculating and total exhaust. Recirculating CACIs filter and circulate air within the unit, allowing for a closed system while maintaining sterility. In contrast, total exhaust CACIs expel contaminated air directly outside, ensuring a higher level of safety by completely removing hazardous materials from the workspace. Each type serves specific operational needs in pharmaceutical settings.</p></p>
