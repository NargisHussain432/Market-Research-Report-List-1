<p><p>Milk cooling systems are essential in dairy processing to maintain milk quality. Horizontal closed tanks are typically designed for efficient cooling and easy cleaning, ideal for small to medium-sized operations. Silo or vertical closed tanks offer a space-efficient solution for larger dairies, allowing for significant storage capacity while minimizing heat exchange. Open tanks, while less commonly used due to hygiene concerns, allow for rapid heat dissipation and are often utilized in smaller farms or for short-term cooling needs.</p></p>
