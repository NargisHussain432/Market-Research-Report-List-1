<p><strong>The Venipuncture Transilluminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Infrared Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The venipuncture transilluminator market is categorized primarily into two types: infrared and infrared fluorescence. Infrared transilluminators use infrared light to illuminate veins beneath the skin, making them visible for easier venous access. On the other hand, infrared fluorescence transilluminators enhance this capability by utilizing a fluorescent dye that binds to blood vessels, producing a brighter and more distinct visualization. Both technologies aim to improve the accuracy and efficiency of venipuncture procedures, benefiting healthcare providers and patients alike.</p></p>
