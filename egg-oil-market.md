<p><ul><li>Liquid-Liquid Extraction</li><li>Supercritical Fluid Extraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Egg Oil market features two primary extraction methods: Liquid-Liquid Extraction and Supercritical Fluid Extraction. Liquid-Liquid Extraction involves using solvents to separate oil from egg yolks, resulting in high purity. This method is cost-effective but may leave residual solvents. Supercritical Fluid Extraction utilizes supercritical CO2 as a solvent, ensuring high-quality extraction without harmful solvents, preserving the oil's beneficial properties. This method is more efficient and environmentally friendly, catering to an increasing demand for natural and sustainable egg oil products.</p></p>
