<p><strong>The Industrial Wireless in Process Automation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wireless in process automation encompasses various communication technologies tailored to different operational needs. Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN) offers high-speed connectivity within a limited area, suitable for real-time data transfer. Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN) is designed for short-range applications, connecting devices like sensors in close proximity. Satellite (GNSS) enables global positioning and communication, ideal for remote operations. Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN) supports long-range communication with low energy consumption, while cellular networks provide extensive coverage and mobility for larger industrial setups.</p></p>
