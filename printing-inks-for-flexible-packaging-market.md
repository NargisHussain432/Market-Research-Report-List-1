<p><strong>The Printing Inks for Flexible Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>UV-curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printing inks for flexible packaging are categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and UV-curable inks. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and ideal for non-toxic applications, using water as a solvent. Solvent-based inks offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various substrates but may release volatile organic compounds (VOCs). UV-curable inks cure instantly under ultraviolet light, providing high durability and vibrant colors, making them popular for high-speed printing applications. Each type serves specific needs in the flexible packaging sector.</p></p>
