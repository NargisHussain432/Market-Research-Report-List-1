<p><ul><li>Gravel Pack</li><li>Fracturing Pack</li><li>Sand Control Screen</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sand Control Equipment market primarily consists of four types: Gravel Packs, Fracturing Packs, Sand Control Screens, and Other solutions. Gravel Packs involve placing a gravel medium in the wellbore to prevent sand inflow. Fracturing Packs use proppants to support fractures in reservoirs, enhancing oil and gas flow. Sand Control Screens are designed to filter out sand particles while allowing hydrocarbons to pass. Other solutions may include chemical consolidants or mesh technologies aimed at preventing sand migration in various applications.</p></p>
