<p><p>The Stirling cryocoolers market comprises two primary types: linear and rotary cryocoolers. Linear cryocoolers utilize a linear motor to drive the piston, providing precise cooling with minimal vibration and noise, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Conversely, rotary cryocoolers employ a rotary design, allowing continuous operation and efficient cooling, suitable for larger systems where space is a premium. Both types are crucial for various applications, including aerospace, medical, and scientific research, where low temperatures are essential.</p></p>
