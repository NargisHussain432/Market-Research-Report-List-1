<p><ul><li>Solvent Base</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-corrosion coatings for the thermal power market come in three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings provide excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for harsh environments but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based coatings are more environmentally friendly, offering easy application and cleanup, though they may require multiple coats for the same protection. Powder coatings are applied electrostatically and cured under heat, providing a thick, durable layer that is resistant to corrosion and wear.</p></p>
