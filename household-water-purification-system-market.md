Ion exchange resin media softens water by replacing harmful ions with less harmful ones. Reverse osmosis membranes offer a thorough filtration process, removing contaminants at a molecular level. UV disinfection uses ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water. Other types may include ceramic filters and physical filtration methods, broadening the purification options available.</p></p>
