<p><strong>The Commercial Specialty Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial specialty tire market comprises mainly radial and bias (crossply) tires. Radial tires feature flexible sidewalls and strong belts that provide better traction, handling, and fuel efficiency, making them ideal for long-haul applications. In contrast, bias tires have layers of fabric arranged diagonally, offering rigid sidewalls and increased durability, suitable for off-road and heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, with radial tires preferred for highway use and bias tires favored in rugged conditions.</p></p>
