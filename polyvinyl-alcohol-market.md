<p><p>The Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) market is categorized into several types based on hydrolysis levels and specific properties. Fully hydrolyzed PVA exhibits high water resistance and is ideal for packaging applications. Partially hydrolyzed PVA offers better flexibility and is often used in textiles. Sub-partially hydrolyzed grades serve niche applications with moderate properties. Low foaming grades reduce foam formation during processing, making them suitable for detergent formulations. Other grades encompass specialized formulations tailored for various industrial applications, enhancing versatility in usage.</p></p>
