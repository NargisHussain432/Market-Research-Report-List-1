<p><p>Social intranet software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. In contrast, on-premises intranets are installed locally on the organization's own servers, providing greater control, customization, and security, which is ideal for businesses with strict regulatory requirements or specific data handling needs.
