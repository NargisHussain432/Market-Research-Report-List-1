<p><strong>The Copper Terminals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blocking Oil Type</li><li>Tube Pressure Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Copper Terminals market can be categorized into two main types: Blocking Oil Type and Tube Pressure Type. Blocking Oil Type terminals utilize an oil-impregnated design, providing resistance to moisture and environmental factors, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, Tube Pressure Type terminals feature a tubular design that facilitates secure connections through applied pressure, ensuring reliability in electrical conductivity. Both types are essential for various industries, depending on specific application requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
