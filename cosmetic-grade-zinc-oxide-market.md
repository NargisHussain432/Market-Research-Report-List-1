<p><p>The cosmetic grade zinc oxide market primarily consists of two types: the indirect process and nano zinc oxide. The indirect process involves a more traditional approach, using higher temperatures to produce larger zinc oxide particles suitable for formulations requiring opacity and UV protection. Nano zinc oxide, on the other hand, features significantly smaller particle sizes that provide enhanced transparency and efficacy in sunscreen and cosmetics, while delivering improved skin feel and broader UV protection. Both types cater to diverse cosmetic applications.</p></p>
