<p><p>The SSL certification market comprises three primary types: DV (Domain Validation), OV (Organization Validation), and EV (Extended Validation) SSL certificates. DV SSL certificates offer basic encryption and validate domain ownership, making them ideal for personal websites. OV SSL certificates require verification of the organization behind the website, providing higher trust for businesses. EV SSL certificates provide the highest level of validation, showcasing the organization's identity prominently in the browser, instilling maximum trust for e-commerce and sensitive transactions.</p></p>
