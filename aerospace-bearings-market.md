<p><p>The aerospace bearings market is segmented into roller bearings, ball bearings, and other types. Roller bearings use cylindrical rollers to reduce friction and handle heavy loads, making them suitable for aircraft landing gears and engines. Ball bearings, which employ smooth balls, are typically used in applications requiring high speed and lower load capacities, such as in auxiliary systems. Other bearings may include needle bearings and thrust bearings, serving specialized roles in various aerospace applications, enhancing performance and reliability.</p></p>
