<p><p>The Lightweight Coated (LWC) Paper market features three main types: Matt LWC Paper, Gloss LWC Paper, and Silk LWC Paper. Matt LWC Paper has a non-reflective finish, ideal for text-heavy publications like magazines and brochures, providing a subtle appearance. Gloss LWC Paper offers a shiny, vibrant finish, enhancing images and colors, making it suitable for high-impact graphics. Silk LWC Paper strikes a balance, offering a semi-gloss surface that combines smoothness with a slightly reflective finish, catering to various printing needs.</p></p>
