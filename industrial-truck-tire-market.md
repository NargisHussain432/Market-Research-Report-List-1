<p><p>The Industrial Truck Tire market comprises three primary types: Pneumatic Forklift Tires, Solid Forklift Tires, and Polyurethane Forklift Tires. Solid tires, made from rubber, offer durability and are resistant to punctures, making them suitable for indoor use. Polyurethane tires strike a balance, offering excellent wear resistance while being lightweight, making them ideal for precise maneuverability in warehouses or distribution centers.</p></p>
