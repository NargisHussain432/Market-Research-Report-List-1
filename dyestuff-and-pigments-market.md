<p><p>The dyestuff and pigments market encompasses various types of dyes and pigments used across industries. Disperse dyes are ideal for synthetic fibers, offering excellent color fastness. Reactive dyes bond chemically with cellulose fibers, ensuring vibrant, durable colors. Sulfur dyes are cost-effective for cotton, providing deep shades. Vat dyes are known for their superior fastness and are used for cellulose and synthetic fibers. Acid dyes are suitable for protein fibers like wool and silk. Pigments are insoluble colorants used in paints, inks, and plastics. Other dyes include specialized types for niche applications.</p></p>
