<p><strong>The Rigid Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Roller Bearing</li><li>Tapered Roller Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid bearings market encompasses various types, notably cylindrical roller bearings and tapered roller bearings. Cylindrical roller bearings are designed to handle high radial loads and moderate axial loads, offering high stiffness and lower friction. In contrast, tapered roller bearings can support both radial and axial loads, making them ideal for applications subjected to heavy and varying forces. Both types are crucial in machinery, automotive, and industrial applications, ensuring efficient operation and longevity of equipment.</p></p>
