<p><p>The demineralized allografts market is categorized into three primary types: gel, putty, and putty with chips. Gel allografts provide a convenient and easily injectable form for localized delivery and scaffold support. Putty allografts, with a malleable consistency, facilitate filling irregular spaces during graft procedures. Putty with chips combines the advantages of putty and granules, offering enhanced structural integrity and osteoconductivity. Each type serves unique surgical needs, supporting tissue regeneration and healing in various orthopedic and dental applications.</p></p>
