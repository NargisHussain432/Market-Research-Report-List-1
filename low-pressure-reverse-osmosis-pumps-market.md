<p><p>The Low Pressure Reverse Osmosis Pumps market is categorized into three main types: Centrifugal Pumps, Positive Displacement Pumps, and Other specialized pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move water, making them efficient for high-flow applications. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, move water by trapping a fixed amount and forcing it through the outlet, providing consistent pressure. Other market types may include hybrid or niche pumps that cater to specific industry requirements, offering tailored solutions for unique reverse osmosis systems.</p></p>
