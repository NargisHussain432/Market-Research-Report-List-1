<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airbrush paint market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based paints utilize organic solvents as a medium, offering vibrant colors and durability but producing strong odors and requiring ventilation. They are preferred for projects needing high durability and glossy finishes. In contrast, water-based paints use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean. They produce less odor and are safer to use, making them popular for hobbyists and indoor applications.</p></p>
