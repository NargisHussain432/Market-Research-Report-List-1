<p><p>The drones for oil and gas applications are categorized into three main types: Fixed Wing, Multi-rotor, and Hybrid Wing. Fixed Wing drones are ideal for large area surveys and offer longer flight times, making them suitable for pipeline inspections and environmental monitoring. Multi-rotor drones are versatile and capable of hovering, which allows for detailed inspections of equipment and structures in confined spaces. Hybrid Wing drones combine the benefits of both, providing extended range and the ability to perform precise maneuvers, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
