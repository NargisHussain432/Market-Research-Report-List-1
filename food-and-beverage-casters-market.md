<p><p>The Food and Beverage Casters market comprises two primary types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature wheels that can rotate 360 degrees, allowing easy maneuverability in tight spaces, making them ideal for carts and equipment in busy food service areas. Rigid casters, on the other hand, have fixed wheels that only move forward and backward, providing stability and support for heavy loads. Both types are essential for efficient operations in restaurants, catering businesses, and food production facilities.</p></p>
