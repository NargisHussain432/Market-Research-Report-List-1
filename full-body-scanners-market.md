<p><strong>The Full Body Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The full body scanners market primarily comprises two types: X-ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-ray scanners utilize ionizing radiation to generate detailed images of a person's body, highlighting concealed items such as weapons or contraband. In contrast, millimeter wave scanners employ non-ionizing radio waves to create three-dimensional images, detecting objects without the use of harmful radiation. Both technologies are widely used in security settings, including airports and public venues, enhancing safety through effective threat detection.</p></p>
