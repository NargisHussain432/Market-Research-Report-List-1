<p><ul><li>Dry-type Air Filter</li><li>Wet-type Air Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Commercial Vehicle Air Filter Market** includes two main types: **Dry-type Air Filters** and **Wet-type Air Filters**. Dry-type filters use paper or fibrous materials to trap dust and particles, offering high efficiency and low maintenance. Wet-type filters, also known as oil-bath filters, use oil to capture contaminants, providing durability and better performance in harsh environments. Both types are essential for protecting engines from debris, ensuring optimal performance and longevity in commercial vehicles, with selection depending on operating conditions and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
