<p><strong>The Benchtop BOD Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manometric Methods</li><li>Colorimetric Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Benchtop BOD Analyzer market encompasses two primary types: Manometric Methods and Colorimetric Methods. Manometric methods measure the change in pressure during microbial respiration to determine biochemical oxygen demand (BOD), offering precise results for water quality assessment. In contrast, Colorimetric methods rely on chemical reactions that produce color changes proportional to BOD levels, allowing for rapid and straightforward analysis. Both methods serve crucial roles in environmental monitoring, wastewater treatment, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
