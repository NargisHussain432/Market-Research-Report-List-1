<p><ul><li>1xN</li><li>2xN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PLC splitter chips are essential components in optical networks, facilitating signal distribution. They are categorized primarily into 1xN and 2xN market types. The 1xN splitter connects one input fiber to multiple output fibers (N), allowing efficient signal distribution to various endpoints. In contrast, the 2xN splitter connects two input fibers to N outputs, providing dual input capabilities while still enabling multiple output connections. Both types are crucial in enhancing flexibility and scalability in fiber optic communication systems.</p></p>
