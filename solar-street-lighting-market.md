<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar street lighting market consists of two main types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, utilizing solar panels to harness solar energy, store it in batteries, and power LED lights, making them ideal for remote areas without grid access. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights integrate with the existing electricity grid, allowing for supplementary energy use and reduced reliance on batteries, enhancing operational efficiency and providing continuous lighting even during extended cloudy days.</p></p>
