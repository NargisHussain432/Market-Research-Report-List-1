<p><p>Cartridge microfiltration is categorized into several types based on membrane materials and applications. PP (Polypropylene) type is widely used for its chemical compatibility and affordability, suitable for various liquids. PES (Polyethersulfone) type offers higher thermal stability and is ideal for biological applications due to its low protein binding. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) type excels in filtering aggressive chemicals and solvents, making it essential for specialized industries. Other types may include mixed cellulose esters or nylon, tailored for specific filtration needs across diverse sectors.</p></p>
