<p><p>The EV charging solution market is categorized primarily into Level 2 and Level 3 charging solutions. Level 2 charging utilizes a 240V supply, making it suitable for residential and commercial applications, providing moderate charging speeds typically used overnight. In contrast, Level 3 charging, also known as DC fast charging, operates on direct current and offers rapid charging capabilities, ideal for public charging stations, enabling significant range replenishment in a short period. Each serves distinct user needs and infrastructure applications.</p></p>
