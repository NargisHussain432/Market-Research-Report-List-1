<p><p>The surgical rasps market is primarily divided into two types: double-ended and single-ended rasps. Double-ended rasps feature a working surface on both ends, allowing for versatile use in various surgical procedures, making them ideal for extensive shaping and smoothing tasks. The choice between these types depends on the surgical requirements, with both playing crucial roles in orthopedic and reconstructive surgeries.</p></p>
