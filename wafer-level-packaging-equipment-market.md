<p><p>Wafer-level packaging (WLP) equipment is categorized into various types based on design and functionality. "Fan-In" packaging involves integrating multiple chips within a compact area, using a central die with fewer interconnections, ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, "Fan-Out" packaging distributes multiple chips across an expanded area, enhancing heat dissipation and connectivity, suitable for advanced semiconductor applications. Other market types may include options like 2.5D or 3D packaging, catering to specific requirements in miniaturization and increased functionality within compact spaces.</p></p>
