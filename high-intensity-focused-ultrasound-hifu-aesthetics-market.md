<p><ul><li>Ultrasound-Guided</li><li>MR-Guided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) aesthetics market can be categorized into two main types: ultrasound-guided and MRI-guided. Ultrasound-guided HIFU uses real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target tissues during treatment, ensuring enhanced safety and efficacy for procedures like skin tightening and fat reduction. MRI-guided HIFU utilizes magnetic resonance imaging to monitor and direct ultrasound energy, providing detailed visuals of internal structures. Both methods aim to deliver non-invasive treatments with maximum precision, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
