<p><ul><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Multi-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aquarium flow pump market comprises two main types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to move water in one simple action, providing a straightforward solution for smaller aquariums or systems with lower head requirements. In contrast, multi-stage pumps feature multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure and flow rates, making them suitable for larger aquariums or complex setups. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing efficiency, power, and application requirements within the aquarium industry.</p></p>
