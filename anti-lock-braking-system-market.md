<p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market encompasses two primary types: pneumatic and hydraulic systems. Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking Systems utilize air pressure to prevent wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing control in various conditions. In contrast, Hydraulic Anti-Lock Braking Systems use fluid pressure to achieve similar outcomes, often delivering quicker response times and improved performance. Both systems are integral to modern vehicle safety, providing better traction and stability on slippery surfaces while reducing stopping distances.</p></p>
