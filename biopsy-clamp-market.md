<p><p>The biopsy clamp market is categorized into rigid and flexible biopsy clamps, each serving distinct medical needs. Rigid biopsy clamps are designed for precise tissue sampling in stable anatomical areas, providing stability during the procedure. In contrast, flexible biopsy clamps offer increased maneuverability, making them suitable for accessing hard-to-reach or irregularly shaped tissues. Both types are essential in diagnostic procedures, ensuring optimal tissue acquisition for accurate analysis while enhancing clinical outcomes in various medical settings.</p></p>
