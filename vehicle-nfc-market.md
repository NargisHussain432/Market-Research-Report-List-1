<p><ul><li>106 Kbit/S Vehicle NFC</li><li>212 Kbit/S Vehicle NFC</li><li>424 Kbit/S Vehicle NFC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle NFC market includes NFC (Near Field Communication) systems operating at different data transfer rates: 106 Kbit/s, 212 Kbit/s, and 424 Kbit/s. These systems enable secure, short-range wireless communication between vehicles and devices like smartphones or key fobs. Lower speeds (106 Kbit/s) are cost-effective for basic functions like keyless entry, while higher speeds (212 Kbit/s and 424 Kbit/s) support advanced applications like secure payments, diagnostics, or infotainment. Faster rates enhance user experience and functionality, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
