<p><ul><li>Liquid Photosensitive</li><li>Uv Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB Photosensitive Solder Resist Ink market is primarily divided into two types: Liquid Photosensitive and UV Cured. Liquid Photosensitive inks are applied to printed circuit boards and undergo a curing process after exposure to light, usually requiring an additional drying step. In contrast, UV Cured inks are cured instantly when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering faster processing times and improved efficiency. Both types enhance the protection and durability of PCBs, while also optimizing design precision and production speed.</p></p>
