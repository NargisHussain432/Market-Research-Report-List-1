<p><p>Styrene Isoprene Styrene (SIS) is a type of thermoplastic elastomer known for its flexibility and durability. The SIS market is broadly categorized into two types: linear and radial. Linear SIS has a straightforward, straight-chain structure, offering enhanced processability and clarity, making it ideal for applications like adhesives and sealants. Radial SIS has a branched, star-like structure, providing superior elasticity and strength, which is beneficial for products like rubber and performance tires. These structural differences influence their specific applications and market demand.</p></p>
