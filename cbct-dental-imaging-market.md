<p><strong>The CBCT Dental Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Image Intensifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CBCT dental imaging market features two primary types of detection systems: flat panel detectors and image intensifiers. Flat panel detectors utilize advanced digital technology to produce high-resolution images and are known for their compact design and efficient data processing, leading to better patient comfort and faster diagnosis. In contrast, image intensifiers amplify the captured images, allowing for enhanced visualization in real-time. Both types serve distinct roles in dental practices, catering to different imaging needs and preferences.</p></p>
