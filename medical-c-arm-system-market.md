<p><strong>The Medical C-Arm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Mobile C-arm</li><li>FPD Mobile C-arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical C-arm system market features two primary types: CCD Mobile C-arms and FPD Mobile C-arms. CCD Mobile C-arms utilize Charge-Coupled Device technology for imaging, providing good quality at a lower cost, making them suitable for various general procedures. In contrast, FPD Mobile C-arms leverage Flat Panel Detector technology, offering superior image quality, faster processing, and reduced radiation exposure. These systems are typically used in more advanced surgical settings, enhancing visualization during intricate procedures.</p></p>
