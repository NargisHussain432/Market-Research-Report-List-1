<p><strong>The Enhanced PoE Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.3af PoE</li><li>802.3at PoE +</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enhanced PoE Switch market is primarily categorized into two types: 802.3af PoE and 802.3at PoE+ standards. The 802.3af PoE standard delivers up to 15.4 watts per port, making it suitable for devices like IP cameras and VoIP phones. In contrast, the 802.3at PoE+ standard offers up to 30 watts per port, supporting more power-demanding devices such as wireless access points and PTZ cameras. This differentiation allows users to choose solutions based on their specific power requirements.</p></p>
