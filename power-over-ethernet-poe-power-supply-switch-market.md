<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) power supply switches are classified into AC and DC switches. AC switches convert alternating current to the necessary voltage for powering PoE devices, primarily used in commercial and residential applications. DC switches, on the other hand, provide direct current, catering to specific electronic devices requiring consistent voltage levels. Both types enable efficient power delivery and data transmission through a single cable, optimizing installations for IP cameras, wireless access points, and other network devices.</p></p>
