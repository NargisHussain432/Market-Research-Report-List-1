<p><p>Magnesium oxide anti-fire boards are classified based on their thickness, influencing their applications in construction and fire safety. Thin boards (under 8 mm) are lightweight and ideal for non-structural purposes, often used in interior partitions. Medium boards (8-15 mm) offer a balance of strength and fire resistance, suitable for wall and ceiling applications. Thick boards (over 15 mm) provide increased durability and fire protection, making them ideal for high-risk environments and heavy-duty applications in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
