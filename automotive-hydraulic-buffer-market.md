<p><strong>The Automotive Hydraulic Buffer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Tube</li><li>Double Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hydraulic buffer market includes two main types: single-tube and double-tube designs. Single-tube buffers feature a single cylinder with a piston separating oil and gas, offering durability and consistent performance under high stress. Double-tube buffers consist of an inner and outer tube, with oil and gas separated in different chambers, providing smoother damping and better heat dissipation. Both types enhance vehicle stability and comfort, with single-tube buffers excelling in heavy-duty applications and double-tube buffers in everyday driving conditions.</p></p>
