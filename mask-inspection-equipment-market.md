<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection Equipment market includes two primary methods for quality control in semiconductor manufacturing: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects individual dice or chips against one another within the same wafer to detect defects, enhancing yield. In contrast, the DB method compares each die to a predefined database of ideal patterns, allowing for the identification of deviations from design specifications. Both methods ensure high-quality output in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
