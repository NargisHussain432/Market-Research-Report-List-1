<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) curable inks are specialized inks that undergo a photochemical reaction when exposed to ultraviolet light, resulting in rapid curing and solidification. This technology offers several advantages over conventional inks, including faster production speeds, reduced solvent emissions, and improved print quality. These inks are widely used in various applications, such as packaging, labels, and commercial printing. </p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Curable Inks Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period. The increasing use of UV inks in the packaging sector is also notable, as they provide excellent adhesion and durability.
