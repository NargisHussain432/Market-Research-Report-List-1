<p><p>The 3D 5-axis laser cutting machine market primarily consists of two types: Fiber Laser Cutting Machines and CO2 Laser Cutting Machines. In contrast, CO2 laser machines use gas lasers, suited for cutting non-metals like wood and acrylic. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing productivity across various sectors.</p></p>
