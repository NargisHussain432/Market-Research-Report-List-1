<p><ul><li>Adherent</li><li>Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viral vaccine cell culture media market is categorized into adherent and suspension types. Adherent media support the growth of cells attached to surfaces, making them ideal for producing certain virus types and vaccines. In contrast, suspension media facilitate the growth of free-floating cells, allowing for higher cell densities and larger-scale production. Both types are crucial for the development and manufacturing of viral vaccines, catering to different cell lines and production requirements in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
