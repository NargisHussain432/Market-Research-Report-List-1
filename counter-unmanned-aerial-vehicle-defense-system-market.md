<p><p>The Counter Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (C-UAV) Defense System market is categorized into two main types: Detection Systems and Detection and Disruption Systems. Detection Systems focus on identifying and tracking unauthorized UAVs using technologies like radar, electro-optical sensors, and radio frequency detection. In contrast, Detection and Disruption Systems not only detect UAVs but also take action to neutralize them. This can involve jamming communications, employing net capture techniques, or utilizing directed energy to incapacitate or divert the drone.</p></p>
